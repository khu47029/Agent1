# Lead-to-Proposal Workflow

1. Collect name, business, business type, location, WhatsApp, goal, budget and timeline.
2. Sales agent qualifies the lead and records missing details.
3. Audit agent reviews the website or online presence.
4. SEO agent adds local SEO and Google Business Profile recommendations.
5. Proposal agent recommends Fast, Growth or Premium using approved prices only.
6. CEO agent checks claims, scope, timeline and tone.
7. Human owner confirms final price and sends the proposal on WhatsApp.

Never auto-charge, promise rankings, or publish private client information.
